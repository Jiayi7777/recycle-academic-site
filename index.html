Transforming Waste Through Education

ReCycle Academy promotes sustainable living through free waste recycling education

"We believe every discarded item holds untapped value. Through knowledge sharing, we transform recycling into art and regeneration into global change."

Our History

ReCycle Academy was founded in 2018 by environmental pioneer Dr. Lily Green. What began as small community workshops teaching plastic bottle transformation and paper crafts quickly grew as interest in sustainable living surged.

Our Policies

Commitment to sustainability and educational excellence

Core Policies

Zero-Waste Operations

All campus operations follow strict zero-waste protocols. 98% of materials used in our workshops are diverted from landfills through our closed-loop systems.

Accessibility Guarantee

All core curriculum is available free of charge. Premium certification programs offer sliding-scale pricing based on income verification.

Community Partnership

Minimum 40% of workshop materials sourced from local waste streams. All community projects prioritize under-resourced neighborhoods.
